About

My name is Sampson Berlinski. I'm a Chemist currently enrolled in an accelerated BS/MS program at Stony Brook University. I do research at the Institute of Gas Innovation and Technology. I'm very interested in environental activism and low-carbon energy technology.

I also do a lot of other stuff on the side, like work for our Student Government, and make digital art. I also love to row, hike, bike, and really anything else that gets me outside and into nature.

If you'd like to reach me, please either use the contact form below or message me on LinkedIn!
